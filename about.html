How I think

I like breaking complex problems into smaller, explainable pieces. Whether it’s data, systems, or people, I care about understanding why something works before deciding how to act.

Analytics, to me, is not about fancy models or dashboards. It’s a language — a way to clarify assumptions, connect evidence to decisions, and move ideas forward with confidence.

What I’ve done

My background spans business analytics, finance, and operations. I’ve worked across market research, financial analysis, e-commerce operations, and marketing support — and I enjoy translating data into insights that are practical, communicable, and grounded in real-world context.
